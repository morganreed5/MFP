MARQUEE FIRE PROTECTION, established in 1989, is a family owned and operated fire protection contractor.
MFP offers a wide range of services to the following areas:
  • Northern California
  • Central California
  • Northern Nevada
As a premier commercial fire sprinkler contractor, MFP has a dedicated team that follows each project from inception and design through construction completion. Our estimators, project managers, in-house designers, and installers are industry professionals ready to provide a functional fire sprinkler system for our customers. We specialize in in-house design, installation, repairs, maintenance, and inspections of various fire protection systems, fire pumps, and standpipe systems.
