Design & Preconstruction

MFP's design team is comprised of a staff of full-time, in-house designers. Every designer possesses the education, accreditation, and/or experience in their field of design.
MARQUEE FIRE PROTECTION utilizes numerous softwares and programs to deliver our projects on time and on budget. These programs allow for three dimensional coordination and clash detection, as well as building information modeling.The programs indlude but are not limited to the following:

  • Navisworks
  • AutoCAD
  • Revit

Our designers collaborate with project representatives, architects and fire district authorities to identify building plan conflicts, coordinate system layouts, and design fire protection systems to meet required codes.
All designs are submitted and approved by the apppropriate fire protection district and/or authority having jurisdiction.
MARQUEE FIRE PROTECTION is proud to employ NICET III and NICET IV in-house designers.

Estimating & Project Management

MARQUEE FIRE PROTECTION has a full staff of estimators and project managers.
These employees are certified and/or trained in fire protection and have ample experience in delivering accurate project estimations, as well as executing skillful project management.
Competitive proposals are submitted to owners or gerneral contractors and, once approved, become jobs managed by the estimating individuals.
Our project managers work in conjunction with contracted clientele, architects, and fire protection districts, in addition to MFP's designers and installers, to assist in the timely and economical completion of each job.

Installation

MFP provides new construction and tenant improvements for various commercial settings such as municipalities, school districts, and hospitals, as well as private and government projects.
Experienced installers and supervisors have conventional and hands-on training of the equipment, codes, and standards specified for each project. Qualified supervisors oversee precise installation of materials and systems.
All apprentices are registered in a 5-year apprenticeship program which includes on-site experience and courses administered and accredited both Federally and by the State of California.
Project managers oversee scheduling and project communication to ensure each job is completedon budget and on time. Additionally, our long standing relationships with many building and fire departments assist us in obtaining timely inspections and systems' approval.

Repairs & Maintenance

MARQUEE provides scheduled and emergency reairs and maintenance for various types of commercial properties.
Our field staff works diligently, utilizing their skilled precision and efficient time management to ensure all services meet our high standards of professionalism and customer care.

Inspections

MARQUEE provides weekly, quarterly, annual, and 7-year inspections and certifications for various types of systems and fire pumps.
We also provide fire hydrant flow testing.
All testing and inspections are performed by trained personnel, and required applicable reports are submitted to owners and fire departments upon completion.
